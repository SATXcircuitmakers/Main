About Us

San Antonio Circuit Makers is a non-profit organization dedicated to teaching electronics, circuitry, and STEM skills to students of all ages. Our mission is to inspire creativity, innovation, and problem-solving through hands-on learning.

Our Programs

We offer a variety of programs, including:

  • Workshops for beginners and advanced learners
  • Summer camps for kids and teens
  • Community outreach events
